How to Get Good

by Carlos Genis

1)Try to select the orbs that are the same color as your character’s type. This will make it easier for your character to super, therefore doing more damage.

2)When choosing a leader select a leader that gives ki to the other units in your team. If your leader gives every character in your team ki, you will find it easier to super attack your enemies.

3)Complete missions to get stones and other rewards such as zeni, (in game currency) training items, and friend points.

4)Learn about type advantages and disadvantages. There’s a chart to help you in the top right corner.

5)Drop lots of money In hopes of good cards.

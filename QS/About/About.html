What is DBZ Dokkan Battle?

by Carlos Genis

DBZ Dokkan Battle is a mobile game developed by Bandai Namco. The mechanics of the game are pretty straight forward, you complete missions for dragon stones and then use those stones to ‘summon’ new units. Each summon costs 5 dragon stones for one character, or 50 stones for 10 characters. You then put those characters in your team and fight the computer with them. As you go through the game, the characters you fight will get stronger. To counter this, you will constantly find yourself summoning, trying to get better units in order to make progress. Use your stones wisely since there’s only a limited amount you get from playing the game. If you want more stones, you will have to open your wallet.
